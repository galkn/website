About

I am a senior software engineer at the data company Trifacta, and live in Berlin, Germany.

Previously, I completed my M.Eng. and B.Sc. at MIT, where I worked as a graduate researcher at the MIT Media Lab Social Computing Group. I developed wearable sensors and analyzed physiological data as part of a research project on early childhood development.

An an undergraduate, I interned with the infrastructure team at Etsy where I built DataFindr, an API-endpoint discovery and documentation tool.
